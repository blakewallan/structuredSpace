Todd Allan

The Person Behind Structured Space

Since I can remember I've had a passion for seeing ways to organize and create order in my environment. From my room in high school with all records sorted by size and in alphabetical order, to creating a complete spare parts inventory with re-ordering template for my garage. I see organizing as design, and am thrilled with seeing the "after" of a project. Our approach to organizing is integrating people with spaces. We provide a complimentary initial assessment to get a feel for the client's current situation and needs. This followed by a onsite strategy meeting, then a detailed project plan with estimated costs completes the initial client interaction. We also provide our clients with ongoing organizational design services to maintain systems established during our work together.
